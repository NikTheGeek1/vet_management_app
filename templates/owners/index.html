This page concerns pet owners. The owners are either registered members in our system or not. Although usually they come with pets, there are cases where an owner might not have their own pet but want to get registerd with us anyway.

Owners can be added using our owner-registration form. After submiting their details, e.g., phone email etc, they are also asked if they want to give us a feedback, or to leave a testimonial.

There is also a list of owners containing all the owners and their details. There, it is possible to edit or delete an owner entry.
