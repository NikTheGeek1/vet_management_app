This page concerns pets. The pets depend on owners, that is, a pet can't exist without an owner, and they have independent relationships with vets, visits, and treatments.

To add a pet one has to go to the Owners page, a pet can't exist on its own.

There is also a list of pets containing all the pets and their details. There, it is possible to edit or delete a pet entry. One can also find information of a pet's history of visits and treatments.
